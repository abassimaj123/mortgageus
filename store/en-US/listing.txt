MortgageUS is the most complete mortgage calculator for the United States — built for home buyers, homeowners, and real estate investors who want accurate numbers fast.

★ 17 POWERFUL CALCULATORS IN ONE APP

🏠 MORTGAGE CALCULATOR
Calculate your monthly payment (P&I), full PITI breakdown (Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance), PMI, HOA, and more. Automatically detects Conforming vs. Jumbo loans based on 2026 FHFA limits ($832,750). PMI auto-cancels at 78% LTV per the Homeowners Protection Act.

📊 AMORTIZATION SCHEDULE
See every payment across your full loan term. Animated yearly breakdown with interest vs. principal split, progress bars, and halfway milestone. Donut chart shows exactly how much goes to interest.

💰 AFFORDABILITY CALCULATOR
Find out how much home you can afford. Uses both the 28% front-end (housing) and 36% back-end (total debt) DTI ratios for conservative and standard estimates. Accounts for existing monthly debts.

➕ EXTRA PAYMENT CALCULATOR
See how much time and interest you save by paying extra each month or with a lump sum. Know your new payoff date and exact interest savings before you commit.

🔄 REFINANCE CALCULATOR
Should you refinance? Enter your current loan, new rate, and closing costs. Get your break-even month and total lifetime savings — no guessing.

📈 ARM CALCULATOR
5/1, 7/1, 10/1 Adjustable Rate Mortgage scenarios. See your payment at the initial rate, after each cap adjustment, and at the worst-case ceiling. Know your rate shock risk before signing.

⚖️ LOAN COMPARATOR
Compare up to 3 loan scenarios side by side — different terms, rates, or down payments. See which option saves the most over the life of the loan.

📐 DTI CALCULATOR
Debt-to-income ratio calculator. See your front-end and back-end DTI instantly — know if you'll qualify before you apply.

🏛️ FHA LOAN CALCULATOR
FHA monthly payment with MIP (Mortgage Insurance Premium) auto-calculated. Upfront MIP + annual MIP split by month. Ideal for low down payment scenarios.

🎖️ VA LOAN CALCULATOR
VA funding fee automatically applied based on service type, down payment, and first/subsequent use. Zero PMI — the full VA advantage shown clearly.

🌾 USDA LOAN CALCULATOR
USDA Rural Development loan: guarantee fee + annual fee calculated automatically. Zero down payment scenarios supported.

🏦 HELOC CALCULATOR
Home Equity Line of Credit: draw period (interest-only) vs repayment period payments. See how rate changes affect your HELOC cost.

💵 CLOSING COSTS CALCULATOR
Estimate total closing costs by state. Breakdown of lender fees, title, escrow, prepaid items, and government fees — no surprises at closing.

🛡️ PMI CALCULATOR
Private Mortgage Insurance cost by LTV and loan amount. See exactly when PMI drops off (78% LTV per HPA) and how much you save.

📈 INVESTMENT RETURN CALCULATOR
Compare buying vs. renting. Opportunity cost of down payment, equity build-up, appreciation, and net investment return side by side.

🔵 MORTGAGE POINTS CALCULATOR
Should you buy down your rate? Enter points cost and new rate — see your break-even month and total lifetime savings.
