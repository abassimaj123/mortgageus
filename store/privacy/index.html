Privacy Policy — MortgageUS

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

MortgageUS ("the App") is a mortgage calculator application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect financial data you enter into calculators — all calculations run locally on your device.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not require account registration.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and SQLite:

  • Your calculation history (loan amounts, rates, results)
  • App preferences (language, theme)
  • Premium status (whether you have purchased the premium upgrade)
  • Ad-free session timestamps (rewarded ad windows)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of MortgageUS displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We delay ad measurement initialization until required consent is obtained.

Premium users ($4.99 one-time purchase) receive a completely ad-free experience.

In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Premium upgrade purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase verification is handled by Google Play's billing system.

Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregated usage data (e.g., which calculators are used most). This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the App.

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized crash reports. These reports help us identify and fix bugs. No personal data is included in crash reports.

Children's Privacy

MortgageUS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Backup

Android device backups are disabled for MortgageUS (android:allowBackup="false"). Your calculation history is not backed up to Google Drive or any cloud service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
