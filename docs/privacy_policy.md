# Privacy Policy — MortgageUS

**Last updated: April 14, 2026**

MortgageUS ("the App") is a mortgage calculator application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Enter

All financial data you enter in the App (home price, interest rate, loan term, income, etc.) is processed **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not store, sync, or have access to any mortgage calculations you perform.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use third-party services that automatically collect certain non-personal technical information:

#### Google AdMob (Advertising)
The App displays ads served by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
- IP address
- Device model and operating system version
- App usage events

AdMob uses this data to serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized ads at any time:
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (off)

AdMob's privacy policy: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### Firebase Analytics
The App uses **Firebase Analytics** to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics such as:
- Number of sessions
- Screens viewed
- Feature usage frequency

This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally. It helps us understand which features are most useful and improve the App.

Firebase Analytics privacy policy: [firebase.google.com/policies/analytics](https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics)

#### Firebase Crashlytics
The App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect crash reports, including:
- Device model and OS version
- App version
- Stack traces at the time of a crash

This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. It does not include any personal or financial information you enter.

Crashlytics privacy policy: [firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics)

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- No name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information (PII)
- No financial account numbers, credit scores, or banking data
- No location data
- No contacts or calendar access
- No camera or microphone access
- No login or account required

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. The only third parties that receive data are the services listed in Section 1.2 (Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics), and they receive only the technical data described above, subject to their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Financial inputs: never stored outside your device
- Analytics/crash data: retained by Google/Firebase per their standard policies (typically 14 months for Analytics)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- Access, correct, or delete data held about you by our third-party partners
- Opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 1.2)
- Request information about data processing

To exercise these rights with Google, visit: [myaccount.google.com](https://myaccount.google.com)
