## Long Description — Google Play (4000 chars max)

```
MortgageUS is the most complete US mortgage calculator for homebuyers, homeowners, and real estate investors. Whether you're buying your first home, planning a refinance, or comparing loan options — get accurate results in seconds.

──────────────────────────────
🏠 COMPLETE PITI CALCULATOR
──────────────────────────────
Don't just calculate P&I. MortgageUS gives you the full monthly payment breakdown:

• Principal & Interest — based on exact PMT formula
• Property Taxes — annual rate, split monthly
• Home Insurance — monthly premium included
• PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) — auto-calculated when LTV > 80%, auto-cancels at 78% LTV (per HPA)
• HOA Fees — optional monthly dues

Supports conventional, FHA, VA (no PMI), and jumbo loans. Automatically detects conforming vs. jumbo using 2026 FHFA limits ($832,750 single-family).

──────────────────────────────
📊 FULL AMORTIZATION SCHEDULE
──────────────────────────────
See every single payment over the life of your loan — all 360 months for a 30-year mortgage, or 180 for a 15-year. Each row shows:

• Monthly payment amount
• Principal portion
• Interest portion
• Remaining balance
• Month PMI drops off

──────────────────────────────
💰 EXTRA PAYMENTS SIMULATOR
──────────────────────────────
Find out exactly how much you save by paying more each month. Enter any extra monthly payment and instantly see:

• Months (and years) shaved off your loan
• Total interest savings in dollars
• New payoff date

Even an extra $200/month can save 7 years and $100,000+ in interest.

──────────────────────────────
🔄 REFINANCE CALCULATOR
──────────────────────────────
Should you refinance? Enter your current loan terms and the proposed new rate:

• New monthly payment
• Monthly savings
• Break-even point in months
• Makes sense indicator (break-even ≤ 84 months)
• Total interest comparison

──────────────────────────────
⚖️ LOAN COMPARATOR
──────────────────────────────
Compare two loan scenarios side by side:

• 15-year vs 30-year
• Fixed vs adjustable rates
• Different down payment amounts
• Monthly payment difference
• Lifetime interest difference

──────────────────────────────
🇺🇸 BUILT FOR THE US MARKET
──────────────────────────────
• 2026 conforming loan limits (FHFA) — $832,750 single-family
• PMI auto-cancel at 78% LTV (Homeowners Protection Act)
• VA loan support (no PMI)
• Supports all US mortgage term options

──────────────────────────────
✅ KEY FEATURES
──────────────────────────────
• Instant calculations — no sign-up, no account required
• Works 100% offline — your data never leaves your phone
• Dark mode support
• Clean, modern interface
• Free to use

MortgageUS respects your privacy. We collect no personal information. No login required.

Download MortgageUS and make your most important financial decision with confidence.
```
